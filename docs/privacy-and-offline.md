## Data privacy and storage

The Charité Imaging Classification is designed to prioritize user privacy and data security:

- **Client-side storage:** All data you enter is stored **locally on your device**. No personal or sensitive data is sent to or stored on a server.
- **Data security:** Keeping data client-side minimizes the risk of data breaches and keeps your data private.

## Progressive Web App (PWA)

ChIC is also available as a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** which can be installed to your home screen and used offline. The PWA works in most modern browsers on desktop and mobile; for the best experience, keep your browser up to date.

### Installing the PWA

1. **Open the tool** in your web browser: <https://halbritter-lab.github.io/ChIC/>
2. **Install prompt** a prompt to _"Add to Home Screen"_ appears in compatible browsers (or use your browser's install/"Install app" menu item).
3. **Confirm installation** to add the app to your device.
4. **Launch from your home screen**, just like any other installed application.

### What the PWA gives you

- **Offline access:** use the tool without a constant internet connection.
- **Faster load times:** a smoother experience after the first visit.
- **Automatic updates:** the app updates itself to the latest version when online.
